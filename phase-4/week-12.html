Week 12 — Capstone: Treasury Insights from Public Data

Phase 4 · CapstoneSkill Focus: All 5~24+ hours
Course Progress
Week 12 of 12

Ship a capstone that demonstrates all five AI co-work skills applied across SQL, pandas, and Tableau. Write a 2,000-word case study pitched at hiring managers. Close out the 12-week course with a complete public portfolio.

Learning Goals

  • Full-stack analysis: data layer (SQL), analysis layer (pandas), presentation layer (Tableau)
  • All five AI co-work skills across one cohesive project
  • Ship: 4-deliverable capstone with optional demo video

Content Releases After Week 11

Full capstone brief, deliverable scaffolds, and demo-video script template unlock after Week 11 commit.

Capstone Brief (Preview)

Is the US Treasury Under Liquidity Stress in 2026?

A fictional CFO of a mid-market manufacturer wants to understand whether there are signals in public Treasury data that should change how they think about cash management over the next 12 months. You are the analyst they hired to answer that.

Deliverables

  1. Data layer (SQL). Load 3+ Treasury datasets into SQLite or DuckDB. Five queries that extract the core time series.
  2. Analysis layer (pandas). Notebook joining sources, computing 5+ derived metrics (spreads, rolling stats, flags).
  3. Presentation layer (Tableau). Published dashboard for a non-technical CFO audience.
  4. Case study write-up (~2,000 words). Problem, approach, findings, recommendations, limitations. Published on portfolio site and LinkedIn.
  5. Demo video (5 min, optional but powerful). Screen-record yourself walking through the analysis. Post to LinkedIn.

Resources (Preview)

  • Your own portfolio (Weeks 1–11)
  • FRED
  • Treasury Fiscal Data
  • SEC EDGAR

After Week 12

A complete portfolio and an active job search. Keep applying. Keep interviewing. Keep adding one polished portfolio entry every 2–3 weeks.
