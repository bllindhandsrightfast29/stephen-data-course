Course Overview

Twelve weeks, four phases: Excel and financial analysis, SQL, data visualization with Tableau, and Python/pandas with a capstone. Each week drills one of the five AI co-work skills listed below. The shape is deliberate: tools plus the habit of commanding an AI to execute, validating the output, and presenting the insight.

The Five AI Co-Work Skills

Every week drills one of these in a new context. By week 12 each has been repped two to three times.

  1. Translate business problem to data question. What are we actually asking?
  2. Command AI to execute. Prompt well, get working code, read it critically.
  3. Validate output against domain knowledge. Does this number even make sense?
  4. Iterate with AI to refine. Push back, correct course, add nuance.
  5. Present the insight to non-technical stakeholders. The story, not the SQL.
