Alex the Analyst Companion

Alex the Analyst's YouTube “Data Analyst Bootcamp” playlist is free and excellent. Use it as a supplement to our curriculum, not a replacement. Here is how his videos map to our weeks.

Tool Translation Note

Alex uses SSMS (SQL Server Management Studio) and Anaconda for his walkthroughs. We use DB Fiddle / SQLite Online and Google Colab. Same skills — lighter, browser-based, cross-platform. When Alex opens SSMS, you open sqliteonline.com. When Alex launches Jupyter locally, you open colab.research.google.com.

Why Use Alex at All?

Three reasons:

  1. Free and high-quality. No course costs anything. Alex's production values and pacing are solid.
  2. Visual learning. Some concepts (joins, pivots, window functions) land faster when you watch someone do them than when you read them.
  3. Bootcamp completion acknowledgment. Alex issues a completion certificate on request after you finish his bootcamp playlist. Not an accredited cert, but a LinkedIn-worthy line.

Why Not Use Alex as the Spine?

Two reasons:

  1. No AI co-work. Alex's curriculum is pre-Claude. His videos teach tools. This curriculum layers AI command and validation on top of the same tools.
  2. Heavier local tooling. Alex installs SSMS, Anaconda, SQL Server, Excel. Our path is browser-first (Claude web app, sqliteonline, Colab, Tableau Public) — faster setup, runs on any machine, no license hassles.
