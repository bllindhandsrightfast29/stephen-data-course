Phase 1 — Foundation

Weeks 1–3Cert: CFI Excel Crash Course

Three weeks to build the habits everything else depends on: AI co-work mechanics, advanced Sheets/Excel, and formal financial analysis vocabulary. Light tooling, heavy reps.

Phase Goal

By the end of Phase 1 you can translate any business problem into a precise data question, execute it in Sheets or Excel with Claude pairing, and validate the output against domain knowledge. This is the foundation. SQL, Python, and Tableau come later and build on top of this.
