Week 8 — Business Dashboards (Ops, Finance, Sales)

Phase 3 · VisualizationSkill Focus: Translate + Present (combined)~15 hours
Course Progress
Week 8 of 12

Build two full business dashboards. Learn wiring: drill-downs, parameters, filter actions. Add Google Analytics Academy cert as resume flex (optional, free).

Learning Goals

  • Advanced Tableau: parameters, filter actions, drill-downs
  • Full round-trip: Skill #1 (translate problem) through Skill #5 (present) in a single week
  • Ship: AR Aging Dashboard + Revenue Performance Dashboard, each with a 300-word CFO brief

Content Releases After Week 7

Dashboard drill-down patterns, CFO-brief templates, and GAIQ prep guide unlock after Week 7 commit.

AI Co-Work Pattern (Preview)

Combination week. Skill #1 at the top: you and Claude turn a fuzzy CFO question into a precise dashboard brief. Skill #5 at the bottom: you iterate the dashboard through 3 rounds of critique. Full round trip, minus the SQL and pandas layers (those come in the capstone).

Resources (Preview)

  • Tableau Public (continuation)
  • Google Analytics Academy — optional GAIQ cert, ~6 hours
  • Kaggle Data Visualization cert (if not earned)
  • Stephen Few “Show Me the Numbers” (classic, used copies cheap)

Mini-Projects (Preview)

  1. AR Aging Dashboard — Chinook or fabricated data, treasury-lead audience, drill-down to invoice detail
  2. Revenue Performance Dashboard — Chinook, sales-style, parameter-driven comparisons

Each gets a 300-word CFO brief and a “how I would deploy this in a real company” paragraph.

Cert Progress

GAIQ (Google Analytics Individual Qualification) earned. Adds a credential that adjacent roles (marketing analytics, web analytics) look for.
