Week 9 — Portfolio Assembly & Job-Search Launch

Phase 3 · VisualizationSkill Focus: Present (Meta Level)~14 hours
Course Progress
Week 9 of 12

Consolidate weeks 1–8 into a public portfolio. Rebuild LinkedIn with a data analyst positioning. Draft master resume plus two tailored versions. Apply to 10+ roles by week's end.

Learning Goals

  • Live portfolio site (GitHub Pages or Notion public)
  • LinkedIn fully rewritten — headline, About, 3 featured case studies
  • Master resume + 2 tailored versions (FP&A analyst, treasury analyst)
  • Ten applications submitted, tracked in a spreadsheet

Content Releases After Week 8

LinkedIn copy critiques, resume templates, and application-tracking worksheet unlock after Week 8 commit. See the Job Search Kit for a preview.

AI Co-Work Pattern (Preview)

Skill #5 applied to career narrative. Claude edits drafts. You write the source material. Key drill: paste a LinkedIn About draft and ask for direct critique on clarity, specificity, and whether each sentence earns its place.

Resources (Preview)

  • GitHub Pages docs (free portfolio hosting)
  • Job Search Kit in this course
  • LinkedIn profile examples from strong analysts in your target market

Mini-Project (Preview)

This week the portfolio is the deliverable. No separate write-up. The portfolio site, LinkedIn rebuild, resume, and first 10 applications are the artifacts.
