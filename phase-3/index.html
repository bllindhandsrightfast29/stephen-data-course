Phase 3 — Visualization

Weeks 7–9Cert: Google Analytics Academy (GAIQ)

Three weeks turning SQL into dashboards people want to read. Weeks 7–8 build Tableau Public dashboards. Week 9 consolidates everything into a public portfolio and launches the job search. You start interviewing at the end of Phase 3.

Phase Goal

Dashboards that pass the ten-second test — a CFO walks past your monitor and can state the headline insight in 10 seconds. Plus a public portfolio site, rebuilt LinkedIn, and 10+ applications submitted by end of Week 9.
