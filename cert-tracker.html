How to Add a Cert to Your Resume

Format as a separate “Certifications” section on your resume and a dedicated “Licenses & Certifications” section on LinkedIn. Include:

  • Cert name (exact)
  • Issuing organization
  • Month/year earned
  • Credential ID or URL if provided
